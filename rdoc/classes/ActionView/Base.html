barcode_to_stream(data)

stream the barcode to the client as a data url. often times, the barcode filesize is so minute, that this is absolutely acceptable. NOTE: I intentionally draw my own img tag for this, image_tag doesn‘t really like this.

get_bytes_from_barcode(barcode, print_options)

this method tricks GBarcode into printing the contents of the EPS into a file pipe, allowing us to get at the binary data, without touching the disk.

[Source]
